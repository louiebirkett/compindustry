Cyber Security for You and Your Family

Cyber Security ensures you and your family are safe from Cyber Crime when using the internet.
All information provided is from the NCSC Website.

Cyber Security keeps your devices safe. Laptops, Smartphones and Computers are all fundamental parts of our everyday lives and all of these devices access the internet and can be victim to dangerous and malicious Cyber Crime leaving you vulnerable.

Use Our tips to help keep yourself and others safe.

Tips

Here is some general Cyber Security advice to remain safe when browsing online

  1. Make use of strong, unique and unguessable passwords for each different account on a differing url.
  2. Ensure Two - factor authentication is enabled which can prevent simple account breaching.
  3. Attempt to use a VPN when connecting to a public Wi-Fi, E.G. McDonald's WiFi
  4. Take precaution when viewing / clicking on emails or message links
  5. Update firmware of device regularly
