Information Security

Fundamentals of Information Security

Information Security is the practice of keeping sensitive information secure from unauthorised access. This may include activities such as modifying, reading, destroying or saving data.

Watch this informative 80 second video by Mavim to understand more.

It is important that the information you interact with is stored securely. This could be sensitive customer details, account passwords or top-secret files. Having these fall into the wrong hands could cause catastrophic consequences. Following the correct procedures is vital. However unfortunately it is not rare that some big organisations fail to do so.
